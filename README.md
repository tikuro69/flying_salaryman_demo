# Flying Salaryman

Flying Salaryman is a small 3D browser game built with Three.js. You guide a salaryman flying above the city, collect `DAY OFF` cards among the clouds, and escape toward the sea after gathering enough days off.

## Play

[https://tikuro69.github.io/flying_salaryman_demo/](https://tikuro69.github.io/flying_salaryman_demo/)

## How to Play

- Move with the arrow keys.
- Touch a `DAY OFF` card to collect it.
- Collect 10 cards to reach the goal.

## Goal

When you collect 10 `DAY OFF` cards, autopilot begins. The salaryman leaves the city behind and keeps flying toward the sea.
